Our Story

La Fay began as a small passion project in a cozy Parisian apartment. Our founder, a perfumer's daughter, combined her love for fragrances with the art of candle making. Each candle is crafted with ethically-sourced ingredients and designed to transform your space into a sanctuary of scent and light.
